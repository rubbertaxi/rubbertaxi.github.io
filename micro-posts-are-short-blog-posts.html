Micro posts are short blog posts. They can be about anything, but most bloggers use micro posts to keep the audience updated on what they are musing, or reading, or a reaction on a recent public event. You can think of them as status updates in Facebook or tweets in Twitter. Micro posts have gained popularity recently as more and more bloggers prefer to have full control of what they post instead of giving it to a third-party content companies like Facebook or Twitter, regardless of how short or long the content is.
