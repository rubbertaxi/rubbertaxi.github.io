How to routinely back up your computer

2017-10-30

If you have ever experienced a data loss, you know how indispensable backups are. They could save hours, if not weeks, of work, invalueable photos, critial documents, etc... Yet, a recent survey showed that more than 60% of computer users do not back up their data, or only do so every 1 year or more.

While backing up your data on a yearly basis may sound good for a bear to record his pre-hybernation diet, it's definitely recommendable to have a more regular backup. So I set out to create a weekly automated, incremental data backup routine with CloneZilla.
