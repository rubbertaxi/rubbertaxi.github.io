Micro posts are short blog posts. They can be about anything, but most bloggers use micro posts to give update on what they are musing, or reading, or a reaction on a recent public event. You can think of them as status updates in Facebook or tweets in Twitter. Micro posts have gain popularity recently as more and more bloggers prefer to have control over the content regardless of how short or long it is than to give it to a third-party content companies like Facebook or Twitter.
